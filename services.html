Services Offered

Below is a list of evaluations offered by my office. If you are unsure about the exact type of evaluation services needed for your client, contact me for a complimentary consultation to discuss how I can best meet your needs.

  • Not guilty by reason of insanity (NGRI)

  • Juvenile resentencing/”second look” for life without parole youth
