Services Offered

Below is a list of psychological evaluations offered. If you are unsure which type of service is needed for your client or case, I offer a complimentary consultation to determine how I can best meet your needs.

Forensic Evaluations – Adults

  • Competency / capacity to stand trial
  • Not guilty by reason of insanity (NGRI)
  • Diminished capacity and sentencing mitigation
  • Civil involuntary commitment
  • Sexual and violence risk assessment

Forensic Evaluations – Juveniles

  • Juvenile transfer / waiver to adult court
  • Sexual and violence risk assessment
  • School-based threat assessment
  • Juvenile resentencing / “second look” for life without parole cases
